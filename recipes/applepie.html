Apple Pie

Description

A classic Bramley apple pie with homemade shortcrust pastry.

Its fruit in a pie so its healthy right? Right?

    Ingredients for the filling:
  • 1kg Bramley apples
  • 140g golden caster sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 3 tbsp flour
    Ingredients for the pastry:
  • 225g butter
  • 50g golden caster sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 350g plain flour
  • Whipped cream
    Method:
  1. Quarter, core, peel and slice apples approximately 5mm thick and lay on baking sheet.
  2. Beat butter and sugar in a large bowl until just mixed. Break in a whole egg and 1 yolk (keep white of 1 egg aside), beat together for 1 minute.
  3. Work in the flour until it starts to clump up and then gather together with your hands.
  4. Work dough into a ball, wrap in cling film and chill for 45 minutes.
  5. Mix sugar, cinnamon and flour for the filling into a bowl leaving enough space for the apples.
  6. Heat oven to 190C fan.
  7. Lightly beat egg white with fork.
  8. Cut off a third of the pastry whilst you roll the rest, use the third to line the pie tin. Roll remaining third to a circle with 28cm diameter.
  9. Tip apples into bowl with cinnamon-sugar mix and briefly mix, then tip into pie tin.
  10. Lay pastry lid over the tin and press edges together to seal. Cut slashes for steam to escape.
  11. Brush with egg white and bake for 45 minutes until golden brown, then sit for 10 minutes.
