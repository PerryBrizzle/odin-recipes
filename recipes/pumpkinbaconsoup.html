Pumpkin & Bacon Soup

Description

The soft, sweet flesh of the Crown Prince pumpkin is perfect for this silky soup, butternut or onion squash are good alternatives.

Get ready for winter with this super warming bowl of hot soup!

    Ingredients:
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 50g butter
  • 1 onion
  • 150g bacon
  • 1/2 Crown Prince pumpkin (or squash)
  • 1l chicken stock
  • 100ml double cream
  • 3 tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • Maple syrup
    Method:
  1. Heat oil in pan with butter.
  2. Add onion and pinch of salt and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Add bacon and cook for 5 minutes until bacon has released its fat.
  4. Reduce heat to medium, add pumpkin and stock and simmer for 40 minutes.
  5. Pour in cream, boil again and remove from heat. Blend remaining pumpkin until smooth.
