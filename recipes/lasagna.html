Lasagna

Description

Take some minced beef, some pasta sheets, some cheese, some other little bits and bobs, and layer it all up.

An Italian favourite, originating in Naples during the Middle Ages.

    Ingredients:
  • Olive oil
  • 750g lean beef mince
  • Tomato sauce
  • 200ml beef stock
  • 300g fresh lasagna sheets
  • White sauce
  • 125g ball of mozzarella
    Method:
  1. Prepare the meat sauce by frying the beef mince in olive oil for approximately 10 minutes, or until browned all over.
  2. Pour over the tomato sauce and beef stock, and season.
  3. Bring to the boil, and simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Heat oven to 180C fan and prepare oven dish.
  5. Spoon a third of the meat sauce into the dish and cover with some lasagna sheets. Drizzle over some of the white sauce.
  6. Repeat until you have three layers of pasta and cover with remaining white sauce.
  7. Scatter torn mozzarella across the top.
  8. Bake for 45 minutes until lightly browned.
